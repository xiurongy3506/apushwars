    President James Polk wanted to expand Westwords. He believes that US has the Manifest Destiny to expand across the cntinent to the Pacific Ocean. Polk wanted the harbor of San Francisco and all the California Territory around it. In october 1845, he sent siplomat John Slidel to purchase the territory between Neuces and Rio Grande rivers for $5 million, California for $25 million, and $5 million for the Mexican land between Texas and California. The Mecian government refused to see Slidel.
